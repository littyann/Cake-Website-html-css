About Us

The Sweet Backery is a sweet local family owned bakery located in San Mateo, California. We pride ourselves with delivering the sweetest treats and cakes to you! All of our cakes and treats are made fresh from scratch with every order. Nothing is ever frozen and we use all natural ingredients. Great care is taken with each and every order, and decorated to impress. We strive to set new trends and are constantly inspired by our customer's creativity! Combining our good ol' fashioned scratch baking with fresh ideas push us into creative unchartered cake decorating territory. We promise to leave you satisfied and full of happiness with every bite!
